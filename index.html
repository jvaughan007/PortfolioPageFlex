Introduction

My name is Joshua Vaughan, and I am a Software Engineering student at Thinkful out of Galveston, TX!

Being a lifelong gaming enthusiast, I enjoy...

  • Working with a team to achieve a common goal
  • Growing and learning from difficult objectives
  • Applying experience to help better the team and achieve victory!

About Me

I began my journey to code during the COVID-19 crisis. After completing a contract position in April of 2020, I decided to pass the time by self-teaching myself web development through FreeCodeCamp and other free resources. This idea came to me when a few friends of mine decided they wanted to create a mobile app. That was the only excuse I needed to learn to code - a chance to work with people as part of a team to create something useful!

One day, during a little longboarding break from my free learning resources, I stumbled upon an ad for Thinkful while scrolling through Facebook on my phone and decided to look into it. After researching for a while, I became determined to make a career change.

Since that day, I have fallen in love with coding - from designing gorgeous and intuitive front-ends to efficient and functional back-end.

If you are seeking a new software developer or engineer, please feel free to reach out! I am willing and able, currently, to assist in projects with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery & React.
